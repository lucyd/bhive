Electronics Play School is a one stop platform for everyone interested in Electronics. Be it pursuing electronics as a career or just a hobby who loves tinkering with circuits, this is the right place for you. Our activities include hands-on electronics sessions in the well-equipped lab, lectures by students and faculty, and various competitions. It is here that you'll get to design your own circuits, learn cool stuff.  The School also has a very active group, which not only keeps the members updated about the activities and advances in electronics but also serves as a forum to solve any technical doubts they may come across and thus broadens their knowledge. And we have a very successful Summer Projects program which is a great opportunity for the students to take up challenging Electronics projects of their choice under the guidance of a diverse mentor group. The projects include Kids project, High School Projects, College projects, Engineering projects, Casual projects, Hobby projects and Industry standard projects.
